# Olist E-Commerce Business Intelligence Dashboard

## Project Overview
This project analyzes the Olist Brazilian E-Commerce dataset to understand the relationship between revenue growth, customer retention, operational efficiency, and customer satisfaction.

Using Python for data preparation and Power BI for visualization, a multi-page business intelligence dashboard was developed to identify key business trends, customer behavior patterns, and operational bottlenecks within a large-scale marketplace environment.

## Business Question
Can strong revenue growth alone sustain an e-commerce business if customer retention and operational performance remain weak?
---


## Business Objectives
- Analyze overall marketplace revenue performance
- Measure customer retention and repeat purchase behavior
- Evaluate delivery performance impact on customer satisfaction
- Identify top-performing product categories and geographic regions
- Generate actionable business insights

## Key Findings
Revenue exceeded 13.5M while repeat purchase rates remained approximately 3%.
Customer retention declined significantly after the first purchase.
Longer delivery times were associated with lower review scores.
Revenue was concentrated in a limited number of categories and geographic regions.
Technology-related categories exhibited premium pricing characteristics.
